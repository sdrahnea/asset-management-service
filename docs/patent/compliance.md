# Patent — Compliance Notes

**Version**: 1.0 | **Date**: 2026-04-04

## Regulatory Context
Patent records typically intersect with:
- National/regional patent office processes and publication rules
- International filing frameworks (e.g., PCT/WIPO, EPO flow)
- Contract/licensing and valuation governance
- Data-protection obligations for personal names in inventorship/analyst fields

## Sensitive Data Areas
### Commercial/financial
- Filing and renewal fees
- Licensing revenue, royalty rates
- Valuation and prosecution costs

### Legal strategy
- Claims scope, limitations/disclaimers
- FTO concerns and blocking potential
- Litigation/opposition history

### Personal data
- Inventor names
- Responsible analyst

If these identify natural persons, GDPR principles apply (purpose limitation, minimization, controlled access).

## Compliance Recommendations
- Keep legal dates and status transitions consistent and auditable
- Enforce uniqueness on legal identifiers (application/publication/grant)
- Track deadline-related fields in a structured cadence
- Restrict visibility of sensitive valuation/litigation fields by role in future iterations

## Current Gaps / Next Steps
- Role-based authorization not yet enforced
- Automated deadline/renewal reminders not yet implemented
- External office synchronization not yet implemented
